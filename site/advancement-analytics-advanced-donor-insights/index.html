How it works

Our Advancement Analytics solution is a three-step process that results in a comprehensive picture of your donor base. As the only solution to combine data analysis with research, each step serves as a foundation to inform and enhance the next one.

Contact Database Augmentation

Update your existing donor database and create a more complete donor profile with additional details including demographic, behavioral, and psychographic information.

Donor Segmentation Analysis

Build on the results of data augmentation and your contact database to create custom donor profiles for even more granular campaign targeting.

Donor Engagement Survey

Reveal the messages and channels that are most effective with a customizable survey to alumni and potential donors.

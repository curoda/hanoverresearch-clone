Still have questions?

Why use Hanover for brand tracking?

Hanover goes beyond traditional brand tracking by combining proprietary analytics with expert strategic support. Our approach identifies the drivers of brand equity and purchase behavior, not just surface-level metrics, while our dedicated team ensures insights are actionable and aligned with your business goals.

What questions can brand health tracking help answer?

Brand Tracker measures key elements of brand health compared to competitors, answering:

  • Who has heard of your brand?
  • What do customers think about you?
  • Who is buying your product, and who isn’t?
  • Are you a preferred brand?
  • Do consumers who engage with your brand intend to purchase?
  • Who are your brand advocates?
  • How do you measure against your competitors?

These insights reveal your complete market position and inform strategic decisions.

What metrics does a brand health tracker measure?

Hanover’s Brand Tracker captures six core metrics across the full brand funnel: awareness (aided and unaided), perception, prior usage, preference, future purchase consideration, and Net Promoter Score (NPS). This integrated framework provides a complete view of brand health revealing how your brand is performing today and where future growth opportunities exist.

How does Hanover reach the right audience for brand tracking studies?

Hanover’s Brand Tracker surveys three key groups: current customers, past customers, and potential future customers. We provide access to 2,000 qualified respondents per survey wave in the U.S. and Canada, with larger or more customized audiences available to ensure you’re gathering insights from audiences most relevant to your business goals.

How often should we run a brand tracking survey?

The right cadence depends on your goals. Many organizations track biannually to monitor market shifts, while monthly or quarterly tracking is ideal for evaluating campaigns or responding to rapid change. For longer-term brand health, annual tracking may be sufficient. Hanover’s Brand Tracker can be customized to the frequency that best supports your strategy and pace of decision-making.

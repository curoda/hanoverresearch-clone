3 Products Made for Automotive DIY Customers

Jacob Musselman April 7, 2025 Uncategorized

Share

DIYers are now more likely to work on their vehicles to either save money or as a hobby, according to a 2024 survey from the Auto Care Association, which uses analysis from Hanover Research. The survey found that 72% of DIYers perform their own maintenance to save money, 48% do it for self-reliance and 42% do it for convenience and time efficiency. To keep your shelves stocked for automotive DIYers, consider stocking these products from Forney Industries.

Multi-Purpose Welder

The Forney 220 MP from Forney Industries is a multiprocess welder capable of MIG, Flux-Cored, Stick and DC TIG welding. It operates on 120- or 240-volt input power and delivers up to 220 amps. This welder features a large digital display, synergic controls and dual gas inputs, making it suitable for a range of materials and applications.

Strip and Finish Disc

The Strip and Finish Disc from Forney Industries is a heavy-duty Type 27 disc measuring 4 ½ inches in diameter with a ⅞-inch arbor. Designed for cleaning and stripping metal surfaces, it features purple nonwoven fiber webbing adhered to a fiberglass backing. The open web design ensures cooler operation and reduces clogging, maintaining cutting effectiveness. Suitable for use on steel, stainless steel and aluminum, it operates at a maximum of 10,000 RPM.​

Battery Charger

The Forney 52750 Battery Charger supports 6V and 12V batteries with multiple charging modes, including 2, 10 and 20 amps. It also provides a 100-amp engine start function for 12V systems. The unit includes LED indicators, safety features, and push-button controls for ease of use across a variety of vehicles and battery types.

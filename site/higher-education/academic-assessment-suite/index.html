Hanover's Academic Assessment Suite

Home > Higher Education > Academic Assessment Suite

Get the data you need to validate an existing or potential academic programs

The Academic Assessment Suite takes the waiting game out of program development, giving you the insights you need to optimize your programs — instantly.

It includes three self-serve dashboards for use across the program development process- from generating ideas for potential new programs to assessing your program portfolio as a whole. You can use all three dashboards throughout the program development process or select the one(s) most relevant to your needs. The suite can be used as a stand alone tool or paired with our custom research for more detailed insight.

The suite uses Hanover’s Higher Education Program Assessment Framework combined with data from degree completions, employment projections, market saturation, and more to measure critical components of academic success and provide instantaneous recommendations on program viability.

Three on-demand dashboards to support you at any point in the program development process

Program Identifier

Identify potential new programs.

Take the first step of program development by narrowing the field of potential programs. Whether you want to winnow a specific field down to a set of the most promising programs or consider blue sky opportunities, Program Identifier generates a short list of potential programs based on student and labor demand metrics.

Features:

  • Review academic programs categorized by High Growth, Emerging, Established, and Low Growth
  • Filter programs within these categories by award level and geography of interest
  • Assess historical growth in student demand and projected growth in employment among related occupations

Portfolio Optimizer

Optimize your existing academic portfolio.

Use the Portfolio Optimizer to evaluate your institution’s current academic program offerings. You’ll gain insights into which of your programs are growing or contracting so you can make resource allocation decisions and decide which programs to provide additional support to, which to maintain, and which to scale back.

Features:

  • View the performance of all your academic programs at once
  • Evaluate the recent growth of your (or your competitor’s) existing program offerings and compare to state, regional, national, and custom benchmarks
  • Identify candidate programs for expansion, adaptation, or re-evaluation

Competitor Saturation

Review a single academic program to determine if it is viable.

Whether you have a single academic program in mind or you are evaluating a short list of potential programs for development or expansion from the Program Identifier or Program Optimizer, Program Reviewer provides a detailed program outlook on a single academic program using student and labor demand metrics as well as job postings.

Features:

  • Validate the case for developing one or more programs of interest
  • Discover detailed data on why the program is a candidate for new development/expansion
  • Receive a concise classification of program outlook as it relates to your specific institution
  • Explore and compare against national, regional, and state benchmarks

Stay ahead of the curve and ensure your academic programs align with the current and emerging interests of prospective students

Hanover’s Program Search Trends Dashboard enables institutions to gather real-time information on the level of interest in academic programs, as measured by the volume of Google searches for those programs.

The result is an early and immediate indicator of student interest in academic programs to guide academic program development, recruitment efforts, and more.

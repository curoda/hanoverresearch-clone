Market Segmentation Identify and target the most viable sectors and sub-markets

Home > Corporate Solutions > Market Analysis > Market Segmentation

Do you have a full understanding of your key market segments?

Customers have unique needs and buying patterns. Dividing your total market into smaller, more cohesive, groups may seem like an overwhelming task, but it enables you to better target your offering and increase sales.

Hanover’s Market Segmentation solutions differentiates existing and potential customers into meaningful groups based on behavioral, attitudinal, demographic, and psychographic data including the response to market relevant factors using quantitative survey research.

How it works: Market Segmentation

Quantitative Survey

Hanover will design, field, and analyze data from a quantitative online survey based on behavioral, attitudinal, demographic, and psychographic data from existing and potential customers.

Cluster Analysis

Survey results are used to cluster or segment the respondents into meaningfully similar groups, based on their responses to questions to identify patterns of behavior or preferences.

Affinity Groups

You belong here

Our Affinity Groups are an employee-led, Hanover-sponsored essential network that focus on creating a culture that is welcoming to all. Open to all Hanoverians, together, they support in recruiting and retaining talent, empowering personal and professional growth, and highlighting opportunities for all Hanoverians to help in giving back to each other and to our communities.

Our Benefits

Supporting the whole you

We’re here to help. With retirement benefits and 401K matching to help you plan ahead, wellness benefits to keep you healthy, and medical coverage to lift you up if you or your dependents get sick, we invest in your well-being now and in the future.

Hanover Research Recognized with 2024 Cigna Healthy Workforce Designation
Hanover Research Awarded Mental Health America Bell Seal for Workplace Mental Health

Flexibility and Time Away

  • Flexible work culture both where (home office, Hanover office) and when you work (the hours of the day)
  • 18 days of Paid Time Off and that’s just the beginning
  • 14 paid holidays including Martin Luther King Jr. Day, Juneteenth, and Indigenous People’s Day, and personal holidays
  • 12 weeks of Paid Parental Leave for birth, adoption, or foster care
  • Additional time off for bereavement, jury duty, and military leave

Health and Wellness

  • Medical including mental health, prescription, dental, vision coverage, infertility, bariatric coverage, weight loss Rx, and adult and dependent orthodontia 
  • Employer contribution to HSA 
  • Employee Assistance Program 
  • 100% Company paid Short Term Disability 
  • Voluntary benefits such as Long Term Disability, Pet Insurance, and Security & Identity Theft  
  • Corporate rates for local fitness clubs 
  • Wellness challenges 
  • Virtual Wellness Week 
  • On-site fitness facilities 

Retirement and Finance

  • 401K investment options 
  • 401K matching program 
  • Free Financial Advisor Services 
  • Flexible Spending Account 
  • Supplemental Life insurance 
  • Pre-tax transportation and parking benefits

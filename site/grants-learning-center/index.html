Benefits for Faculty

  • Deepen knowledge of grant funders and programs with interactive exercises
  • Enhance grant writing and grantsmanship skills

Benefits for Administrators

  • Track usage and engagement with training content across faculty

How it works

Enroll Faculty

Determine which individual or groups of faculty members with approved research concepts would benefit from Hanover’s Grants Learning Center.

Deliver Targeted Training

Faculty progress through modules focused on a specific funding source at their own pace, incrementally building toward a proposal draft in a Hanover-recommended template.

Track Progress

Monitor faculty progress through an implementation pulse check-in survey and analysis of usage and engagement data.

Enhance Mastery

Review progress and recommended next steps with an experienced Hanover advisor who supports your institution’s training.

Strategic Planning and Governance

Turn Your K–12 Organization’s Vision into Measurable Results

Home > K–12 Education > Strategic Planning and Governance

Improve systemwide outcomes with a clear, actionable strategy

Hanover empowers K–12 leaders to drive meaningful change through strategic planning. We help you build a data-informed strategic plan, align resources with community priorities, implement key initiatives (including AI integration and governance), track key performance indicators (KPIs), and strengthen accountable decision-making.

Design a Path to Improvement
Transforming Strategic Plans Into Results: A K–12 Action Planning Toolkit

Everything you need to design, measure, and achieve your strategic improvement goals

Strategic Planning Advisory Services

Develop a coordinated vision and actionable plan

Hanover advisors build your team’s capacity across all phases of strategic planning, from defining organizational statements to setting goals and KPIs, engaging your community, and monitoring action plans.

Strategic Priorities Diagnostic Survey

Assess performance against strategic priorities

Our survey and deep analysis evaluate how your current strategic priorities align with performance outcomes to identify trends, gaps, and next steps.

Strategic Planning Community Engagement Survey

Build consensus around shared priorities

Our survey and deep analysis help you understand how your community perceives your organization to align priorities with strategic goals and resource planning.

Student Outcomes Data Analysis

Gather evidence about organizational needs

We examine performance trends across your academic, behavioral, and program outcomes to identify gaps and opportunities that inform strategic goal setting.

Strategic Planning Community Focus Groups

Increase consensus and strategic alignment

Our team conducts focus groups with key members of your community to capture experiences and identify strategic priorities.

KPI Monitoring Data Dashboard

Track strategic progress and performance

Hanover develops a detailed dashboard with your academic, behavioral, and operational data to easily measure KPI and goal progress and make timely, transparent course corrections.

Strategic Planning Infographics

Communicate strategic plan goals and process

Hanover’s experts develop engaging infographics and messaging to inform community members about your goals, progress, timelines, and opportunities for input.

Educator Learning Center

Invest in training that aligns with strategic priorities

Our core and custom professional learning modules and implementation support help staff develop continuous improvement skills and practices to achieve strategic change.

K–12 Education Grants

Build K–12 Grant Funding Strategies

Home > K–12 Education > K–12 Grants

Fill K–12 funding gaps with strategic grant prospecting and competitive grant proposals.

As education funding shifts, districts and schools need to know how to maximize their grantseeking capabilities. Hanover helps K–12 organizations to access new streams of revenue from private or public sources with flexible, capacity-building services. Our experts can scale up your existing grant activities by providing more comprehensive prospecting and grantwriting support.

Grantseeking Capacity

Prepare your organization for grant success

Hanover helps to build organizational capacity to pursue and manage K–12 grant funding through training, strategic assessment, and benchmarking.

Proposal Support

Bolster grant proposals with expert advice

Our proposal support services include reviewing and revising proposal narrative drafts to increase their chances of success.

Pre-Proposal Support

Plan for effective grant results

Our experts help K–12 organizations develop responsive project concepts, navigate complex funder requirements, and build relationships to ensure competitive grant submissions.

Funding Research

Match needs with strong funding prospects

Our team identifies and evaluates grant opportunities aligned to K–12 projects and funding needs while facilitating planning through funded project research and forecasting.

Proposal Development

Submit winning proposals

Hanover leads grant proposal writing in close coordination with K–12 organizations to deliver polished, timely submissions.
